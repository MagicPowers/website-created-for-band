Our History

Raised in the lap of London Luxury, a young Leila was whisked away from a life of afternoon tea and lordships, to the feral and wild hills of Donegal. It was here in this desolate wasteland that she developed a taste for the blues. The rest is herstory.

Drifting from profession to profession and location to location, Leila's life was like a ship circling a dreaded malmstrom. Experiencing the life of an actor, barista and musician in Northampton, as well as being an idle wanderer across the desert of America, fuelled Leila's ability to sing emotively, an ability that did not go unnoticed. After many years in exile she returned to her home land to the news that the prominent Irish celebrity, Imelda May, had endorsed her, and thus started Leila's time in BIMM.

It was during her first year in BIMM that Leila was given the tools with which to successfully navigate the life of a musician, no longer would she fall prey to shady talent agents. It was here too that she met her Healers, together they were an unstoppable force that ravaged the rebellious city of Dublin, gigging from Squats to Castles, from Festivals to Pubs, and everything in between.
